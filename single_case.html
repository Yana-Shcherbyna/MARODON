SILENCIO Product Promo

Launching a Revolutionary Sliding Door System Using AI

Brand name:
Innovative Sliding Solutions Provider
Industry:
Aluminium Partitioning and Sliding Door Systems
Campaign Goal:
To create an innovative, high-impact promotional video that highlights Silencio’s ability to eliminate disruptive noise, using modern AI technology
Services:
Creative Product Launch Video Campaign

The brand

In the dynamic world of interior solutions, where innovation and practicality collide, a leading Australian manufacturer has emerged as a pioneer in top-tier sliding systems. Hailing from Australia, this company has redefined how spaces are crafted, delivering aluminium partitioning and sliding door systems that stand out for their excellence. At the forefront of their offerings is Silencio, a groundbreaking sliding door system that leads the industry with its exceptional acoustic isolation, boasting an impressive RW36 rating. Designed to silence even the most disruptive environments, Silencio represents the pinnacle of soundproofing technology, making it a go-to choice for offices, homes, and commercial spaces seeking tranquility without sacrificing style. This Australian innovator recognized the need for a product that not only performs but sets a new standard, and Silencio delivers just that.

To introduce this game-changing system to the market, the company sought a bold campaign that would highlight its unique value. They turned to us to craft a promotional narrative that would showcase Silencio’s ability to transform noisy spaces into havens of peace. “We wanted a launch that would make people feel the difference silence can make,” their founder, an architectural visionary, explains. “It’s about more than doors—it’s about creating environments where people thrive.”

There was a MATCH, and our values aligned with the company that gave us this order. VALUES: ambitious / trend-savvy / professional / open to new solutions / experimenting with formats.

Why did our values align?
Because they are innovative and the first in the door market in Australia, and we came up with an innovative approach and move forward with technologies that are currently trending in the media market.

The brand

For Silencio’s launch, the company wanted a strong brand mascot: a lion. But this lion wasn’t an adversary—it was a symbol of noise, representing the disruptive sounds their system was designed to eliminate. The original idea: Feature a real lion storming an office, only to be silenced by Silencio.

The problem:

  • Logistical nightmare – Training, permits, and safety concerns.
  • Ethical concerns – Using live animals could spark backlash.
  • Time & cost issues – A real lion would exceed budget and the 4-week deadline.

We considered traditional 3D animation (like The Chronicles of Narnia) but rejected it—it risked looking too artificial, polished, and disconnected from the campaign’s raw energy. The challenge? Create a lion that felt alive, engaging, and emotionally compelling—all within a tight timeline.

The Solution

Added details on AI approach

To meet the campaign’s ambitious goals, we turned to cutting-edge AI technology to create a hyper-realistic lion —a solution that was faster, cheaper, and more visually striking than traditional CGI.

Instead of manually designing a 3D lion from scratch, we:

  • Trained AI models on high-resolution lion footage for lifelike movements and textures.
  • Used generative AI to create realistic fur, muscle motion, and facial expressions.
  • Integrated AI-assisted animation to ensure the lion’s interactions with the environment felt natural.

Why AI? This approach delivered a lion that was photo-realistic, emotionally expressive, and cost-effective—without the risks of live animal filming.

The Process:

  1. Research & Strategy Analyzed viral video trends to craft a storyline that captivates modern audiences. Mapped out key engagement triggers for social media virality.
  2. Script & Storyboarding Wrote a concise, high-impact script balancing humor and product focus. Developed a storyboard mapping out the lion’s journey from chaos to silence.
  3. AI-Driven Lion Creation & 3D Environment Design Trained AI models on lion footage to achieve hyper-realistic motion and expressions. Designed a 3D office environment that felt authentic using AI-enhanced textures and lighting.
  4. Motion & Editing Seamlessly animated the lion’s movements, ensuring it felt organic in the office setting. Assembled the final video with precise pacing for maximum emotional impact.
  5. Sound & Visual Enhancement Designed a soundscape that transitions from chaos to silence, amplifying the Silencio effect. Finalized color grading & post-production for a polished, cinematic look.
