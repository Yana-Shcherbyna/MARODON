Our Story

Marodon emerged from a clear market necessity—the need to bridge disconnected marketing disciplines.

We consistently observed organizations caught between two unsatisfactory options: creative agencies that delivered visually impressive but commercially ineffective work, and marketing consultancies that produced strategically sound but forgettable campaigns. This division created a pervasive problem—marketing that was either creatively distinctive but strategically hollow, or strategically sound but creatively indistinguishable. We established Marodon to resolve this fundamental challenge. Not through superficial collaboration between separate disciplines, but through structural integration of typically disconnected functions.

Our organizational model eliminates conventional boundaries. Performance analysts work directly with creative directors. Strategic planners collaborate continuously with production specialists. This architectural integration produces work that unites strategic rigor with creative distinction—resolving the compromise that frustrates so many marketing leaders. The outcome is a fundamentally different type of marketing partnership that unifies traditionally separated disciplines, approaching your business objectives as our professional mission while delivering work that achieves measurable impact without sacrificing memorability.

The Solution

Added details on AI approach

To meet the campaign’s ambitious goals, we turned to cutting-edge AI technology to create a hyper-realistic lion —a solution that was faster, cheaper, and more visually striking than traditional CGI.

Instead of manually designing a 3D lion from scratch, we:

  • Trained AI models on high-resolution lion footage for lifelike movements and textures.
  • Used generative AI to create realistic fur, muscle motion, and facial expressions.
  • Integrated AI-assisted animation to ensure the lion’s interactions with the environment felt natural.

Why AI? This approach delivered a lion that was photo-realistic, emotionally expressive, and cost-effective—without the risks of live animal filming.

The Process:

  1. Research & Strategy Analyzed viral video trends to craft a storyline that captivates modern audiences. Mapped out key engagement triggers for social media virality.
  2. Script & Storyboarding Wrote a concise, high-impact script balancing humor and product focus. Developed a storyboard mapping out the lion’s journey from chaos to silence.
  3. AI-Driven Lion Creation & 3D Environment Design Trained AI models on lion footage to achieve hyper-realistic motion and expressions. Designed a 3D office environment that felt authentic using AI-enhanced textures and lighting.
  4. Motion & Editing Seamlessly animated the lion’s movements, ensuring it felt organic in the office setting. Assembled the final video with precise pacing for maximum emotional impact.
  5. Sound & Visual Enhancement Designed a soundscape that transitions from chaos to silence, amplifying the Silencio effect. Finalized color grading & post-production for a polished, cinematic look.
