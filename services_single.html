Why choose our digital marketing and creative agency?

Integrated Intelligence

We've dismantled the traditional agency structure where creative teams and strategic teams operate in separate universes.  At Marodon, your data analysts sit with your designers. It means that insights don't get lost in translation, and creative inspiration is anchored in business reality.

Client Partnership Model

We've thrown out the rulebook and replaced the typical client-vendor dynamic with a partnership model instead. Transparent communication about what's working and what isn't, collaborative problem-solving rather than order-taking, and a shared investment in outcomes. Your success metrics are our success metrics!

Cross-Pollinated Expertise

Our team members are cross-trained across lots of different marketing functions. Your social media strategist knows how to use conversion rate optimisation. Your copywriter gets analytics. Which is great for seeing opportunities others might have missed.

Adaptive Execution

Markets evolve. Algorithms change. Attention patterns shift. Our work evolves with all of the above. We build flexibility into all campaigns, monitoring performance signals to refine approach without waiting for quarterly reviews. Your marketing will roll with the punches as a result.
